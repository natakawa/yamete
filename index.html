About YAMETENFT

What is YAMETENFT?

"Yamete"（やめて） means "Stop it!" or "Quit it!" in Japanese - a classic phrase from Japanese meme culture, anime, and even NSFW contexts.

Combined with NFT, it creates a satirical cry into the void: "Why are you releasing these JPEGs again? Stop it!!!"

Why it's a hit:

Meme Culture: "Yamete kudasai~" is a recognizable meme across the internet. Even those who don't know Japanese recognize the vibe.

Satirical Meta: Perfectly satirizes the overproduction of NFTs. A contract that literally begs not to be minted.

Meta-Art: Each subsequent token visualizes an increasingly desperate "やめて" cry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the meaning behind YAMETENFT? +
YAMETENFT combines the Japanese word "Yamete" (Stop it!) with NFT to create a satirical commentary on the NFT market. It's a self-referential joke about the proliferation of NFT projects.
How many tokens will be in the collection? +
The initial release will feature 10 tokens, each representing an increasingly desperate plea to stop creating NFTs. Future expansions will be determined by community interest.
Is this a serious project or just a joke? +
It's both! While YAMETENFT is built on humor and satire, it's also a legitimate art project exploring the meta-narrative of NFT culture and the irony of digital scarcity.
