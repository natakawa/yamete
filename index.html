About YAMETENFT

YAMETENFT is the world's first collection of NFTs with Stockholm syndrome. Each token starts by resisting ownership but eventually grows to accept its fate.

Created by a team of developers who asked their AI assistant "what's the worst possible use of blockchain technology?" and then actually built it.

Our tokens are minted on a proprietary blockchain that runs exclusively on the tears of our developers and the confused sighs of people trying to explain what we do.

Frequently Avoided Questions

What is YAMETENFT?

YAMETENFT is a collection of digital assets that would rather not be collected, thank you very much. "Yamete" means "stop it" in Japanese, which is what our NFTs are constantly whispering into the void of the blockchain.

Why would I want an NFT that doesn't want me?

Why do people climb mountains? Why do people eat spicy food that hurts them? Humans are weird. Also, each token comes with a unique digital certificate of guilt.

Is this a joke?

We've invested $4.2 million in venture capital and spent 18 months developing this project. So yes, it's absolutely a joke, but an expensive one.

What utility do these NFTs have?

Utility? In this economy? They make excellent digital paperweights and conversation starters. Also, each NFT comes with a unique way to disappoint your parents when you explain what you spent your money on.
